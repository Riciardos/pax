class pax.plugins.peak_processing.RejectNoiseHits.RejectNoiseHits(config_values, processor)[source]¶

Bases: pax.plugin.TransformPlugin

Remove hits in channels with many lone hits from peaks without many other hits. Works with a penalty point system (see #126): * The more lone hits you see in a channel, the more points a channel gets. * Channels with more than a (fairly low) number of penalty points are called suspicious.

For these, we’ll test every peak as follows: * A peak scores points for every pe in non-suspicious channels. * Hits in channels with more penalty points than the peak’s score are removed from the peak.
